Your Turn App

Your Turn is a revolutionary management app created for university apartment turnovers created to become the only needed digital clipboard for the short term fast paced process. Extensively researched, the app organizes and propels all involved workers to prevent losses, increase productivity, communicate responsibilities directly, report emergencies, assign work, and synchronize across multiple workplaces in real time.
